How I Work

The development process
I build and run.

01

Concept & Sourcing

Category identification, supplier evaluation, commercial feasibility. Nothing enters the pipeline without a clear margin target and a defined customer.

02

Specification

Written specs covering dimensions, materials, finishes, tolerances, and acceptance criteria. Built with the production team; measurable and factory-executable.

03

Sampling & Iteration

Multiple prototype rounds with written, structured feedback. Market testing with real customers. Gate decisions made on data, not instinct alone.

04

Production Readiness

QC criteria defined before production starts. Weekly cadence reviews. Clear handoff standards so channel teams can execute without returning to product.

05

Launch & Feedback Loop

Complete launch packs. CX and returns data fed back into the pipeline. The system improves with every cycle.
